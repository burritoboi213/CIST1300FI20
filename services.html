Haircuts

All haircuts are scheduled in 45-60 minute blocks of time. Please note that some haircuts require additional time, such as thicker/longer hair or big changes.

If you'd like to have your hair styled with hot tools such as a Flat Iron or Curling Iron, please request at the time of booking and we will accomodate.

Styling: Professional Blow Dry

$45 and Up

The experience begins with a thorough consulation followed by the perfect product combination and style. For heat styling with a curl iron or flat iron please request at the time of booking. Additional $10 will be added to your service.

Styling: Up-Do

$65 and Up

Each experience begins with a thorough consulation followed by a customised Up-Style of your choice. Note: Up-dos do not include a Blow Dry. Please do not wash your hair on the day of the service in order to allow for maximum friction and hold.

$75 and Up
